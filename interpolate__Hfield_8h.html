Interpolate the x-component of the magnetic field to the centre of Yee cell i,j,k.

Interpolation for the magnetic field must be done across two dimensions. The stored Hx-component at position (i, j+Dy, k+Dz) is associated to the Yee cell (i,j,k). To interpolate Hx to the centre of cell (i,j,k), we must therefore interpolate in the y-direction, then the z-direction, or vice-versa.

Parameters
[in]Hxy,HxzSplit components of the Yee cell
[in]i,j,kYee cell index
[in]nJ,nKNumber of Yee cells in the j,k directions respectively
[out]HxInterpolated value of the Hx field at centre of Yee cell i,j,k
