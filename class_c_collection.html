Detailed Description

A class to encapsulate collection of algebraic terms in the discretized forms of Maxwells equations for E fields. The symbol chosen in the original reference is .

Algebraic terms defined in Section 4.2 of Munro, P,. "Application of numerical methods to high numerical aperture imaging", 2006, PhD thesis, Imperial College London.

The definitions are equations 4.13, 4.14 (pp 82-3). Part of Maxwell's E-field equations in equations 4.7-9.
